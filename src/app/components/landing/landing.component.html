About Real Time Captioning

Originally starting out as a proof concept, Real Time Captioning was created to solve the various issues that concern closed captioning duirng a classroom session. Previous methods used older inefficient technologies that made the classroom experience unpleasant for both the student and the captioner. A student would have to rely on a captioner's laptop to view the closed captions and sit next to them in class, making them feel isolated from their peers. Moreover, transcripts were not readily available which meant students would not have access to the classroom material.
